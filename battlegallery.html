First ever fight, against dimachaerus

This was the first ever fight tht Fred fought in. You can see the moment our bot struggles to get under dimachaerus, because our drivetrain functionality had decreased. In the fight, the front plate gets torn off, rendering our bot essentially useless. Before our opponent lined up for the final shot, we tapped out but he still went through with the hit, as the cage lights did not go red. The design of the bot is being modified to prevent such instantaneuos damage and incapacitation as we faced in our only match.

Low Power Functionality Test

This is a brief clip from part of the functionality testing for Fred. Through testing I found that lower power on the weapon led to more of a flipping type of hit, while higher speeds led to a more saw-like cutting hit. In this example the forks and front plate work excellent against the stationary ~2.5 lb piece of wood which it was tested against, flipping it a few feet into the air.
